How it works

Three steps. Zero guesswork.

HarmoniQ uses a perceptual model trained on how the human ear experiences harshness. It's not a static EQ. It learns your track.

01

Select a profile

Vocal, Drums, Bass, Guitar, Synth, or Mix Bus. Each profile knows exactly where harshness lives for that instrument and sets the detection range automatically.

02

It learns your track

Adaptive mode builds a spectral baseline of your audio in 2 seconds. From that point, it only acts on frequencies that spike above what's expected. The character stays. The problems go.

03

Watch it work

Coral curtains descend on the spectrum to show exactly where and how much HarmoniQ is reducing. Turn up Depth to push harder. Hit LISTEN to hear only what's being removed.
